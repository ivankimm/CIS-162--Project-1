Kitty

This is my cat, Kitty. He is the first cat we got back when I was in High school. He came unexpectedly to us after my dad came home one day and said, "Oh yea, I brought a cat". His bipolar personality used to make it hard to decipher whether he was trying to be sweet or just bite us. Years later after being neutured, he is more sweet and enjoys kisses. He makes a good cuddle buddy and will follow you around.

Pelusa

This is our most recent adpotee. My mom named her "Pelusa" which means fluff is Spanish. As a kitten, she was very shy and stayed close to her mother. After she was separated from her mother, she became more playful with the other cats in my house. She has a sweet personality, she seldom bites or acts aggressive. In the mornings, she likes to run around everywhere with our other cat "Rosalia". She is always sweet, awake or asleep.
